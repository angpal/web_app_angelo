About West Coast Auto

If you like excellent customer service, quality Used Cars and fast and professional Car Servicing, you can’t go past our used car dealership in Perth.

Here at West Coast Auto, we’ve been providing first-class used cars to the Perth region since 1975. We’re proud of the excellent reputation we have built over the past 40 years, and love being known for our trustworthy advice and affordable prices.

Our car yard is brimming with a fantastic ange of cars to choose from including Sedans, Hatches, small 4 cylinder automatics and 4x4's just to name a few. All of our vehicles are sold with genuine safety certificates and statutory warranty, and for your peace of mind we also offer extended warranties of up to 3 years. We have a Queensland transport approved workshop on site which allows Vaughan to offer his customers ongoing servicing at very competitive prices, so we can take care of your needs today and into the future.

Our friendly team are committed to helping our customers through every step of their used car purchase. And if it's finance you are after, Vaughan can assist with that as well. Finance applications can be taken on site through our finance broker (TAP).

If you have any questions about our used cars or services, or if you're looking for a good deal then call in and see Vaughan and his staff today, who will be only to happy to take care of your needs.
